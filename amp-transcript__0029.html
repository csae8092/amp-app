Lunch with W.H.A. at the Opern-Café on
13th' Feb.1968 .

Talk of awful impasse in Vietnam
War; W. so afraid of use of nuclear
weapons that he can hardly bear to
read or listen to the news. Feels US
will have to get out;perhaps to
blame for the "Pueblo" affair & wars
in territorial waters.
May have to vote Republican, though
he's never done so before,because the
Democrats really have no one for
President.
Says the Hippies are moving in on
his district. I asked what a hippy
does when he gets old; W. said thats'
What he asks them; too soon to tell .
Told him about Hundertwasser &
his naked protests.
I sSaid I was glad he had told
British young people that L.S.D is
a dead duck for creative workers .
W. told me at some length about
his trials of LSD &mescalin,
Point is that young people need
to discover who & what they are .-

become unimportant. Curious
effect of listening to music -
intolerable as the sounds lose

Basically, you achieve a mild

did a lot of harm by publishing

they went round to local pub.

at him & thought "this is it.-
Later postman said "I waved
at you , why didn't you answer?"
