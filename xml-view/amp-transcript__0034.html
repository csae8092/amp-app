I have<lb></lb> put togther a complete collection of Auden's first editions, selections from<lb></lb> his contributions to periodicals, representative anthologies, his films for<lb></lb> the G.P.O Film Unit, many items connected with his work for the Group Theatre,<lb></lb> all the opera libretti, <del><unclear></unclear></del>Britten's arrangements of his poems and many other items.<lb></lb> I have also arranged for various portraits of Auden to be sent to Vienna ; they<lb></lb> include Sir William Coldstream<unclear>'</unclear>s 1937 portrait, a portrait by Mervyn Peake painted<lb></lb> in the same year, two drawings by Maurice Field and a portrait by Don Bachardy.<lb></lb> I have also managed to get 26 lithographs by Henry Moore which he did in 1973 to<lb></lb> illustrate a selection of Auden's poems.<lb></lb> Stephen Spender has agreed to come to Vienna during the<lb></lb> period of the Exhibition - that is 11th October to 3rd November. Humphrey<lb></lb> Carpenter will lecture on Auden at the invitation of the British Council, so it<lb></lb> all promises to be a splendid celebration of Auden's life and work.<lb></lb> I was terribly disappointed not to have seen you when I was<lb></lb> in Vienna in March but alas I was at the mercy of my friends who have cars and,<lb></lb> as luck would have it,they were all so busy at various things I was left stranded in<lb></lb> Vienna throughout my brief visit. However I shall be spending September, October<lb></lb> and November in Austria and I very much look forward to seeing you then.<lb></lb> I had a letter from Edward Mendelson yesterday and he asked<lb></lb> me to send you his very best wishes which I do along with my own. I hope this<lb></lb> finds you in good spirits and that Summer is <del><unclear></unclear></del> better behaved in Austria <del><unclear></unclear></del>than<lb></lb> it is here in Ireland.<lb></lb> With all good wishes,<lb></lb> Yours very sincerely,<lb></lb> Mich<unclear>ae</unclear>l <unclear>O'Sullivan</unclear><lb></lb> MICHAEL O'SULLIVAN. </p> <pb facs="https://iiif.acdh.oeaw.ac.at/amp/amp_0232/" type="sheet"></pb> </div> </body> </text> </TEI>
